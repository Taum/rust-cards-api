---
description: "Rules for writing down plans"
alwaysApply: true
---
- When in plan mode, write down your plan into the plans/ folder of the sub-project being worked on.
- Number the plans by incrementing a 2-digit prefix.
- Ignore plans starting with X, they don't fit in the project development timeline.
- Write down the plan **EXACTLY** as submitted. You can only make small changes if you encounter errors during implementation, then note what was actually implemented.
